Perched Precariously

By patrickg, 29th October 2020

My family has a way with cups. They love putting them on the edge of counters/tables and I have grown accustom to pointing this out for rectification. I did it so much, that I made it fun for myself to say it and try to alleviate some of my nagging by saying “that object is perched precariously”, which when accompanied with a finger point, usually is enough to get the issue dealt with.

I made this site to capture that unique experience so it can be shared around the world.

Click anywhere to hear my beautiful deep voice.
